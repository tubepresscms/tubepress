# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in TubePress — and sorry to be blunt: **this repository does not accept contributions.**

It is a read‑only publishing mirror maintained by the TubePress team:

- **Pull requests are closed automatically** by a bot, without review.
- **Issues and discussions are disabled.**
- The development tree is private; there is no public roadmap process here.

## Where to go instead

- **Questions & support:** [tubepress.io/contact](https://tubepress.io/contact)
- **Documentation:** [tubepress.io/documentation](https://tubepress.io/documentation)
- **Security reports:** see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)
- **Release notes:** [tubepress.io/changelog](https://tubepress.io/changelog) or the Releases tab here

We do read feedback sent through the site — feature ideas and bug reports are genuinely welcome there.
